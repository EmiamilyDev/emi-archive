#001 IN STYLE Fashion Week

First International Fashion Week

March 2025 Paris, France Miu Miu

The Story

Emi was invited to attend her first international fashion week as a guest of Miu Miu, marking a significant step in her growing presence within the global fashion industry. This moment represented more than a runway appearance—it symbolized the beginning of a new chapter.

Walking the Miu Miu runway in Paris, Emi embodied the brand's aesthetic perfectly. The collection featured minimalist silhouettes with warm gold accents, and her presence brought a distinctive elegance to the presentation. The moment captured photographers' attention and became a trending topic across fashion media.

This appearance would go on to open doors for future collaborations and brand partnerships, establishing Emi as a respected figure in the international fashion scene.
